ABOUT US

The Murga Bhavan’s roots can be traced all the way back to 1989. Right from the beginning, we always emphasized on taste and ensured that the food made at his hotel was both delicious and unique. This was achieved and still is by meticulous selection of ingredients prepared from quality masala products.

High quality and taste are of prime importance, and because of this our food products have attained widespread popularity all across South India. There are numerous articles published in different journals about our quality and taste. As a result of our efforts, the word “Muruga Bhavan” for a common man. in South India today refers to High Quality Food.

Muruga Bhavan has been delighting people from the rich to the poor for the last 30 years and the journey goes on!!!
